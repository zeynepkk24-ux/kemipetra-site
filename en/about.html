About Us

Trustworthy chemistry, hands-on technical support

Kemipetra brings together the clarity, speed and engineering support that coating, oil & gas, water treatment, food-pharma and cosmetic manufacturers seek in raw material sourcing — under one roof.

Our approach

Not a supplier — a stakeholder in your process

Every raw material on your production line directly affects product performance and shelf life. So our job isn't "give a price and ship"; it's defining the right specification with you, discussing alternatives, delivering complete regulatory paperwork and staying connected after shipment.

We were founded to remove the language, logistics and documentation friction Turkish manufacturers face when sourcing from global suppliers. Today we regularly bring in 200+ raw materials from 30+ countries, with flexible quantities ranging from small trials to container shipments.

Our values

How the work is done matters as much as what gets done

🎯

Right specification

No "more or less the same." CAS number, purity, particle size, viscosity — every parameter shapes formulation performance, and we control accordingly.

📦

Continuous availability

Local stock for frequently used raw materials and a backup supplier plan for critical items. Avoiding line stoppage is our priority.

📑

Complete documentation

SDS, COA, TDS, REACH declarations, halal/kosher/vegan certificates — whatever you need to have ready in an audit, delivered with each shipment.

🔬

Engineering dialogue

A counterpart who speaks your formulation team's language. We work proactively with alternative material, cost reduction and shelf life suggestions.

🤝

Long-term partnership

We aim for multi-year partnership, not single transactions. Therefore we don't compromise on quality under price pressure — and don't recommend that you do either.

⚖

Transparent operation

Origin, producer, customs process and price components are clear. If there is any unexpected delay or quality risk, we communicate it openly and on time.
