01 — Procurement

Procurement & Logistics

Production lines can't stop. That's why we don't depend on a single supplier for critical raw materials — we work with a verified producer network. Frequent items are kept in our local stock; for special items we set up a planned shipment calendar.

  • ✓
    Multi-sourcing: At least two approved producers for every critical material.
  • ✓
    Local stock: 24-48 hour delivery on frequent items.
  • ✓
    Flexible quantities: From 1 kg sample to 20-ton container.
  • ✓
    Customs & logistics: Import, storage and last-mile delivery from a single hand.
  • ✓
    Shipment tracking: Transparent communication from order placement to door delivery.
02 — Regulatory

Regulatory & Compliance

A raw material being "usable" depends on the right paperwork accompanying it. We deliver the documents demanded in audits, exports and customer reviews with every shipment, and run the dossier process for complex regulatory submissions with our team.

  • ✓
    Standard pack: SDS (16 sections), COA, TDS — with each shipment.
  • ✓
    REACH: Registration status verification for manufacturers exporting to Europe.
  • ✓
    Cosmetic notification: Dossier preparation support for CPNP and Türkiye notification processes.
  • ✓
    Food & pharma: Full documentation for food grade, USP/EP, GMP-certified materials.
  • ✓
    Halal/kosher/vegan: Guidance on certified alternatives and follow-up of paperwork.
03 — Technical

Technical Solutions

Sometimes the need isn't simply "ship me material X" — it's defining the problem correctly. If your formulation isn't behaving, you have a shelf life issue, or you're searching for alternatives under price pressure, our technical team speaks the same language as your formulation team.

  • ✓
    Alternative raw materials: Equivalent product suggestions balancing performance and cost.
  • ✓
    Formulation support: Starter recipes and dosage usage guides.
  • ✓
    Sample policy: Free technical samples for trials before production.
  • ✓
    Application testing: Special test support via direct connection to producer laboratories.
  • ✓
    Site visits: On-site technical review for complex processes.
